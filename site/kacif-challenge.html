About the Challenge

If 250 individuals make a gift to any area of GW by June 15, they will unlock a $25,000 gift to the Knowledge in Action Career Internship Fund (KACIF). All donations enhance the GW experience and ensure that students can access every opportunity.

Each year, hundreds of students take advantage of real-world education and training through internships, but the financial burden can be challenging. KACIF provides a stipend so that students can accept a once-in-a-lifetime unpaid internship and launch their future careers.
